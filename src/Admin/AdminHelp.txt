How to upload Images:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You are allowed to update first and second pictures of the gallery page:
suggested link to upload your images: https://imgbb.com/
Suggested photo size: width=930px, height=900px
It is a free image hosting and sharing service, upload pictures, photo host.
uploading instruction on imgbb.com:
1-Select image 
2-Select Don't auto delete 
3-Upload image for free
4-Embed codes: select HTML full link 
(e.g. <a href="https://ibb.co/1TLKmsN"><img src="https://i.ibb.co/XtbpCyP/pic10.png" alt="pic10" border="0"></a>)
5-Get src for img from html link and enter in image box (e.g. https://i.ibb.co/XtbpCyP/pic10.png)

How to upload videos:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1-instagram: open your video on instagram, and copy the URL on the browser
(e.g. https://www.instagram.com/p/B9Xnus_nTau/)
you should add word "embed" at the end of the URL(e.g. https://www.instagram.com/p/B9Xnus_nTau/embed)
its ready to enter in the admin page.

2-YouTube: open your video on youtube,click on "SHARE" link, choose "embed"
you see a long code, you need just the src part:
src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/2p2hC--loq4"
enter the address below in the admin page:
https://www.youtube.com/embed/2p2hC--loq4
